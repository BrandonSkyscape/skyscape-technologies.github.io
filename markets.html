Housing

Additional housing units

Market: £13.5b

Many of the world's cities are facing housing crisis and shortage of essential facilities for its citizens. Utilising rooftop spaces to install additional flat units cheaply and quickly can assist cities with coping housing crisis in the short term. Likewise, converting rooftops into public playgrounds, nurseries or care homes can greatly benefit its citizens that cannot access such services due to their acute shortage. Urban farms that provide locally sourced food to consumers and businesses find rooftops an ideal location for their operations.

Infrastructure

Improving wireless connectivity and accelerating internet of things

Market: £13.5b

Despite access to internet has become an indispensable need in the developed world, many cities struggle with internet speeds and call for better infrastructure. Rooftops are ideally positioned for wireless infrastructure to be placed to provide a more stable and faster acccess to internet to the cities inhabitants. The fast approaching 5G revolution will find streamlined access to rooftop spaces a great catalyst in its roll-out. And how about smart rooftops? Would it be useful to place some IoT devices up in the air?

Transportation

Accelerating integration of urban air mobility

Market: £13.5b

Utilising airspace above our cities and freeing ourselves from the limitations of ground transport is the next logical step. Opening up and adapting rooftops for such revolution is crucial and will result in more efficient movement of people and goods around our cities. We want to support companies developing drone delivery networks as well as hugely ambitious projects of VTOL (vertical take-off and landing) vehicles aiaming to allow people to travel rooftop to rooftop.

Environment

Expanding systems that actively clean urban air

Market: £13.5b

Over 7 million deaths are caused annually by air pollution related illnesses and 1.48 trillion EUR is lost due to polluted air in Europe only. Despite pollution is our major problem, there are not many proactive air cleaning schemes being installed across cities. Fitting rooftops with air cleansing technology, converting rooftops into environment-friendly areas can greatly reduce our impact on the environment and save lives of thousands of people every year.

Energy

Installing decentralised energy generation and storage systems

Market: £13.5b

The cost of decentralised energy systems is falling rapidly whilst the increasing and continued growth may potentially transform the energy industry from a national market regulated by the government to a new market where integrated energy solutions are determined by consumers, businesses and communities. Rooftops provide an ideal platform for expansion of such systems within cities and even in rural areas. We should not waste our precious and scarce land on static infrastructure which can be placed on top of our buildings.
