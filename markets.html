Housing

Rooftop space developments

UK Market: £51bn

With 66% of the world's population destined to live in cities many, such as London, are facing a housing shortage and are experiencing unnecessary urban sprawl into green belt areas. Rooftop space developments have an untapped ability to not only cope with the increase in housing demand, but to promote sustainable city living and reduce demands on other crucial infrastructure services.

Wireless

Connectivity and IoT

UK Market: £14bn

Access to reliable and fast internet connection has now become the standard. The UK’s investment in the 5G network will catapult our cities into the next generation of the internet age and has been estimated to generate an additional £7bn per annum in new business for the economy. Due to the technical complexities of the 5G, more antennae (in line of sight) are needed for the roll out of this technology making rooftops ideally positioned for installation of 5G antennae and various IoT devices. At skyscape, we identify and help to address barriers which could slow or prevent 5G deployment.

Transportation

Preparing for urban air mobility

Global Market: £11.27bn

Utilising airspace above our cities and freeing ourselves from the limitations of ground transport is the next logical step. Planning for the transportation infrastructure of the future by adapting existing rooftops and designing new buildings suitable for such a revolution is crucial. At Skyscape we want to work alongside companies (such as Airbus, Lilium, Volocopter) developing drone delivery networks and air taxis to catalyse their integration in our cities.

Agriculture

Growing food locally in urban farms

Global Market: $161bn

Apart from the creation of small businesses, rooftop urban farms have many interrelated community and societal benefits. Locally sourced produce reduces the need for excessive preparation, packaging, storage, shipping and associated charges. Rainwater attenuation is achieved through the reduction of hard surfaces that contribute to our urban flooding issues. Rooftop urban farms increase building insulation thus reducing building energy costs and CO2 generation. Biodiversity and food security promoting produce variety in our urban environments.

Energy

Decentralised energy generation and storage

UK Subsidies: £7.6bn

The UK government's goal of reducing CO2 by 57% by 2030 through (beside others) generating 25% of the UK’s energy from solar alone is looking more and more achievable as the UK economy embraces decentralised energy systems. Urban rooftops provide an ideal platform for expansion of such decentralised systems. We should not waste our precious and scarce land on static infrastructure which can be placed on top of our buildings.

Environment

Air cleansing technology

UK Market: £26m

At Skyscape we promote the creation of green spaces and proactive air cleaning schemes being installed along the cities' most polluted routes. Our algorithms identify most efficient rooftop locations for air cleansing technology to be placed. Converting rooftops into environment-friendly areas can greatly reduce our impact on the environment and save lives of thousands of people every year.

Community

Market: unknown

Community pollinates every opportunity provided by Skyscape. The challenges of urban living are not only those of air pollution and energy generation but the creation of Healthy Streets and environments designed to tackle the physical inactivity crisis, the creation of jobs and social spaces aimed at promoting positive mental health.
